About Me

Who Am I?

Hello there,

I am a copywriter/content manager with relatively little experience but an overwhelming desire to create well-researched and highly-readable content on the Internet. Nobody wants to read flat information - everybody wants to see a lot of thoughts, expertise and care in each text found online.

How did my career of a copywriter start?

3 years ago, being an English teacher (as required by my diploma), I realized that I would like to keep quiet for a while. I wanted to read something (besides students' works) or write on paper myself. I started freelancing and enjoying the writing process at the same time.

This way, I started to specialize and succeed in English writing, proofreading and editing. Now, I can develop SEO-friendly content with a view to B2B and B2C marketing strategies.

I have written content over a variety of different types (essays, research papers, blog articles, technical specifics of electronic devices, and other non-fiction pieces of writing).

I am experienced in working with HTML tables and WordPress.

I'm eager to work on new interesting projects where my research, writing and editing skills, and my creativity will come in handy.
