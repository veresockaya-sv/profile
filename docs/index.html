About Me

Who I Am

Hello there!

I'm a copywriter/content manager with 3+ years of experience in creating various types of content - web pages, blog posts, SEO-friendly texts. Now, I'm engaged in the content creation from A to Z. My priority #1 is to create well-researched and highly-readable content so that the Web could be full of useful and interesting information. I'm sure that nobody wants to read flat information - everybody wants to see a lot of thoughts, expertise and care in each text found online.

How did my career start?

At first I was an English teacher (as required by my diploma). Once I realized that I would like to be silent for a little while. I wanted to read something (except for students' essays) or write on paper myself. I started freelancing and enjoyed the writing process at the same time.

This way, I started to specialize and succeed in English writing, proofreading and editing. Now, I can develop SEO-friendly content with a view to B2B and B2C marketing strategies.

Today, I have a full understanding of the fundamentals of content marketing, SEO and creative copywriting. Besides, I have good time management skills and self-organization.

I'm experienced in working with HTML/CSS, WordPress, Tilda, Google Analytics and basic SEO tools.

I'm eager to work on new interesting projects where my professional skills and my creativity will come in handy.
